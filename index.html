Welcome to pig game!

The first player that gets to the TARGET SCORE wins!

TARGET SCORE:

Insert a target score to start playing!

Instructions

1. Select the target score. The target score is the score a player has to get to win

2. Roll the dice

3. If you roll a one you lose your turn. If you roll any other number you accumulate it in your "current score"

4. If you want to store your current score you have to hold

5. When your hold score gets to the target score, you win!
